NFL Week 10 Tips

Prices supplied by Betfocus.

Atlanta vs Indianapolis

Kyle Pitts Sr. Over 42.5 Receiving Yards — $1.9
Indianapolis has consistently struggled to contain tight ends, allowing the second-most receiving yards to the position this season. Their linebackers grade poorly in coverage and the safeties often give up separation across the middle. That’s where Pitts does his best work, and he’s been Atlanta’s most reliable option against man coverage. With projected targets trending up and a favourable defensive matchup, Pitts is well positioned to clear this mark.

Colts -5.5 — $1.9
The Colts arrive in strong scoring form and now add elite corner Sauce Gardner to a secondary that already competes well. Atlanta’s run game has stalled, their offensive line is banged up, and late-game execution continues to hurt them. With better balance, quarterback play, and red-zone efficiency, Indianapolis should control the tempo and cover this number in Berlin.

Baltimore vs Minnesota

Ravens -4.5 & Over 48.5 Total — $3.6
Baltimore’s offense looked sharp with Lamar Jackson and Derrick Henry working in rhythm, and they match up well against a Minnesota defense missing key secondary pieces. The Vikings can still move the ball with J.J. McCarthy and their receiving weapons, so they should contribute to the total, but the Ravens have the stronger consistency and maturity in late-game moments.
Prediction: Baltimore 31–24 — both the spread and total in play.

Buffalo vs Miami

De’Von Achane Over 30.5 Receiving Yards — $1.9
Miami continues to scheme Achane into open space as both a runner and receiver. He owns one of the highest RB target shares in the league and runs more routes than almost any running back. Buffalo’s linebackers have struggled with speed out of the backfield, making this another game where short passes and motion-based design should get Achane well involved. Very achievable number.

New England vs Tampa Bay

TreVeyon Henderson Over 72.5 Rush + Rec Yards — $1.9
With Rhamondre Stevenson out again, Henderson should handle the bulk of touches. His explosiveness was clear over the last two games, producing efficient yardage both on the ground and through the air. Tampa Bay is stronger defensively up front, but the expected volume in all phases gives Henderson multiple pathways to hitting this total.

New Orleans vs Carolina

Panthers -5.5 — $1.9
Carolina have found rhythm, winning three of their last four as Bryce Young continues to look more composed. Rico Dowdle’s return brings balance to the offense and home-field advantage has been meaningful recently. New Orleans is transitioning, starting a rookie QB and dealing with several key injuries. Carolina’s form and stability give them a clear edge to cover at home.

Arizona vs Seattle

Seahawks -6.5 & Trey McBride Anytime TD — $3.6
Seattle’s defense is generating high pressure rates with just their front, making this a difficult matchup for Jacoby Brissett behind a struggling offensive line. Lumen Field is also a tough road environment, especially on a short week. Meanwhile, Trey McBride has become the focal point of Arizona’s red-zone offense, scoring in every Brissett start and leading all tight ends in red-zone targets.
Seattle to control the game, McBride still likely to find his opportunity.

Detroit vs Washington

Jahmyr Gibbs Anytime Touchdown — $2.5
This is a strong bounce-back spot for Gibbs after a tough matchup last week. Washington’s defense ranks poorly, especially in red-zone efficiency, and they’ve allowed multiple scoring drives with limited resistance. Gibbs has been Detroit’s most dynamic scorer and had TDs in 16 of his last 20 prior to the Minnesota game. Excellent chance to get back on the board.
