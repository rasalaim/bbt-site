Friday 31/10/25 – 7:30pm

Hawks vs Breakers @ WIN Entertainment Centre

The Hawks found their rhythm again with a gritty one-point win over the Wildcats, and JaVale McGee was a major factor at both ends. The Breakers struggled at home last week and now face a tougher road matchup. With momentum back on their side, the Hawks should control this one.

Prediction: Hawks by 7

Saturday 1/11/25 – 5:30pm

JackJumpers vs Taipans @ Silverdome

The JackJumpers have been the more consistent side and should assert themselves at home. Even if the Taipans come in with momentum, the JackJumpers’ system, depth and home-court edge give them the upper hand. Take the line early before it shifts.

Prediction: JackJumpers by 7

Saturday 1/11/25 – 8:00pm

Bullets vs Kings @ Brisbane Entertainment Centre

This is a key response spot for the Kings after back-to-back losses. Brisbane at home is always a challenge, but Sydney has too much talent to keep slipping. Expect Jaylen Adams to come out aggressive and drive the Kings back toward the top four conversation.

Prediction: Kings by 5

Sunday 2/11/25 – 2:30pm

Phoenix vs Wildcats @ John Cain Arena

Phoenix continue to outperform expectations and sit firmly in the contenders’ group. The Wildcats are trending the wrong way and may struggle to keep pace, especially on the road. Phoenix are playing with confidence and should cover.

Prediction: Phoenix by 7

Sunday 2/11/25 – 4:30pm

36ers vs Hawks @ Adelaide Entertainment Centre

The Hawks are being underrated in the market. McGee’s interior presence is a real matchup issue for Adelaide, and the Hawks’ recent win over Perth should lift belief. Expect a tight contest where taking the points makes sense.

Prediction: Hawks by 2

Monday 3/11/25 – 7:30pm

United vs Kings @ John Cain Arena

United remain flawless at 9–0 and have the benefit of a full preparation week. Sydney’s inconsistency makes this a difficult spot for the Kings. United’s structure, discipline and shot creation should hold strong again at home. The line still looks light.

Prediction: United by 9
