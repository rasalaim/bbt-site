Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about BestBettingTips.com.au

Welcome to the BestBettingTips.com.au FAQ. Here, we explain how we provide racing and sports tips, cover player and team performance trends, and review licensed Australian bookmakers. Our aim is to offer clear, research-based insights so readers can stay informed when following their favourite sports and racing events.

Are your tips and previews free to read?
Yes. All tips, previews, form discussions, and commentary on BestBettingTips.com.au are free to access. There are no subscriptions, paywalls, or sign-up requirements.
Do you provide NBA player performance insights?
Yes — during the NBA season, we publish player performance notes and matchup breakdowns, including trends in scoring, rebounds, usage rate, and opponent defensive style. This content is for informational and analytical purposes, helping readers understand key drivers behind on-court performance.
How do you review bookmakers?
Our bookmaker reviews are conducted independently by our editorial team. We evaluate licensed Australian wagering operators based on: platform usability, market range and depth, customer experience elements, and responsible gambling tools available. We also consider publicly available user feedback. No bookmaker can pay to influence their ranking or review outcome.
Are your bookmaker rankings independent?
Yes. Rankings are based on objective criteria and editorial evaluation — not advertising priority. All operators listed are licensed in Australia.
Which sports and racing codes do you cover?
We publish tips and analysis across a range of major events and competitions, including:
  • Horse Racing & Greyhounds
  • AFL & NRL
  • NBA & NFL
  • Soccer (EPL, A-League, Champions League)
  • Cricket, Tennis, Golf & more
Coverage varies throughout the year depending on season schedules.
How often are tips updated?
We publish tips daily, usually in the morning. Horse racing tips are updated based on each day's race meetings. Sports tips are published ahead of key fixtures and games throughout the week.

Responsible Gambling

BestBettingTips.com.au supports responsible gambling. Our content is informational only and not intended to encourage wagering. If gambling is affecting you or someone you know, call Gambling Help on 1800 858 858 or visit gamblinghelponline.org.au.
